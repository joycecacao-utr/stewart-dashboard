How these metrics are defined

AI Resolution — Engaged Stewart chats resolved with no human escalation (no agent/transfer keywords detected in transcript). Bounce sessions excluded from denominator.

Stewart Bypass Rate — % of Freshdesk tickets from customers who never touched Stewart (no Stewart_AI tag). High bypass = the biggest untapped automation opportunity.

CSAT — Freshdesk post-ticket satisfaction rating: Happy responses ÷ total rated tickets. Excludes Voiceflow auto-evals.

SLA Attainment — % of tickets with first response within SLA: Urgent ≤1h · High ≤4h · Normal ≤8h · Low ≤24h (calendar hours, approximation). Update thresholds in fetch-data.js.

Avg First Response — Mean calendar hours from ticket creation to first agent reply (Freshdesk, General queue).

Stewart Bounce Rate — Welcome-only sessions (user never sent a message) ÷ all sessions. Kept separate so bounces never inflate AI Resolution.

Stewart Cost — Sessions × $0.05/session. Update COST_PER_SESSION in fetch-data.js to match your Voiceflow bill.

Cost per AI Resolution — Total cost ÷ AI-resolved sessions. Shown against ~$6.00/human ticket for ROI context.

Date ranges — All metrics aggregate from daily rollups over the selected window. Data is retained for the last 60 days; longer ranges show all available days.
